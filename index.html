About Us

NOT A GYM.
A BATTLEFIELD.

Spartan Calisthenics Park is Ahilyanagar's premier outdoor calisthenics training ground. We believe the human body is the only equipment you'll ever need — no machines, no memberships chains, just raw movement mastery.

Whether you're starting from zero or chasing advanced skills like muscle-ups and handstands, we train you the Spartan way: disciplined, progressive, and powerful.

  • Outdoor training environment
  • Bodyweight-only methodology
  • All fitness levels welcome
  • Expert coaching by @calisthenicsamey
